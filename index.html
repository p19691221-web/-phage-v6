PHAGE v6

PHAGE v6 is WOKE GLOBAL's AI Governance Infrastructure platform. It provides a control layer for AI Agents and automated systems, enabling identity verification, governance enforcement, auditability, risk control and post-quantum security.

What is PHAGE?

PHAGE is an AI governance control platform that helps enterprises track what AI Agents did, why they did it, whether the action followed policy, and whether the action can be audited and explained.

Why PHAGE?

Verify AI Agent identity, ownership and permission scope.
